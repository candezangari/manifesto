Logics of Emotions

We won’t be able to fight terrorism if we don’t understand terrorists.

Terrorists not only pursue the goal of killing us, they also pursue the goal of separating us.

We should be able to empathize with people who are leaving it all to get out of their country.

We are naturally intolerants.

Education sometimes overestimates numbers or objectives.

It is not about teaching statistics, but about teaching critical thinking.

A Fragmented World

You can't deposit your faith in government to solve our problems. You have to get out there.

Being so connected, we end up differentiating more.

Someone’s terrorist could be someone else’s freedom fighter.

Terrorism is the second biggest illegal business. Who finances these groups? Who trains them?

Unity is the ultimate tool against terrorism.

How can we improve that part of the world that is outside the maps?

Internet is blurring the limits of the state.

Human Technology

We don’t take full advantage of all existing probabilities, we stick to what’s empty.

We become social activists on many occasions, but only through social media. We give likes and we don’t really discuss topics. As millennials we must understand the channels at our disposal.

Do something extraordinary with the daily. You don’t necessarily have to cause an impact on thousands.

We must forget about the fantasy of Internet’s freedom.

We need to highlight the importance of fighting for human rights in media.

Independent to political reasons, access to information should be free. Internet is one of the most democratic means of communication.

A Conversation Across Borders

  • Frontiers are mental limits

  • Crisis emerge out of rejecting what is different

  • Freedom of expression must be a previous step to dialogue

  • Internet helps us to think freely. If we want to build a better society, we must represent all the different personalities that exist
